Get to know who I am

I work at the intersection of eCommerce operations, catalog quality, and performance advertising. I like fixing things that block sales and building flows that make future launches smoother.

I have hands-on experience in Amazon catalog operations, troubleshooting defects, analysing search terms, and supporting multi-region launches. Most of my work sits between category, account management, and tech teams.

I enjoy taking messy data or broken processes and turning them into clear, repeatable playbooks. From fixing high impact catalog issues before a sale event to improving ROAS by cleaning search terms and restructuring campaigns, I focus on measurable wins.

I am currently a Media Specialist at Flywheel Digital, and I am open to roles in Product Operations, eCommerce Operations, and Ads or Growth.
