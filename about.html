About Sahil Pandita

eCommerce operations · Catalog & product support · Amazon Ads · Reporting

I work at the junction of catalog quality, fulfilment, and advertising. My main focus is making sure products are ready to sell, easy to find, and supported by clean data and clear reporting.

Over the last few years I have worked on Amazon marketplace accounts across regions, fixing catalog issues, preparing launches, and supporting PPC teams with audits and reporting.

I like structured, boring processes that quietly prevent problems. Most of my work is about finding the weak points in a workflow, building a simple checklist or template, and then tracking it so the team does not have to think about it again.

How I like to work

  • • Start with the data and the defect tickets, not guesses
  • • Write down small playbooks so others can repeat the fix
  • • Keep communication clear with short updates and screenshots
  • • Align with product and tech before making changes in bulk
  • • Measure impact in simple numbers, not huge decks

What I can help with

  • • Cleaning and standardising product catalogs before peak events
  • • Setting up launch checklists for new products or brands
  • • Supporting PPC teams with search term audits and negatives
  • • Building simple weekly reports in Excel or Sheets
  • • Working with cross-functional teams to close open defects

If your team is dealing with broken catalogs, launch chaos, or unclear ad reporting, I would be happy to walk you through my case studies and see if I can help.
