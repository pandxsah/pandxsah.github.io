Case Studies

A few real examples of problems I worked on across catalog, fulfilment, and Amazon PPC. These are written in a simple Problem → Action → Result format so they are easy to skim.

Catalog · Operations

Operational defect fix for high priority SKUs

Attribute mismatch, broken search visibility, and fulfilment errors across a small but important set of SKUs.

  • • Mapped defect tickets to specific attributes and templates
  • • Standardised key fields and aligned with product and tech
  • • Re-uploaded in controlled batches with checks

Result: Reduced repeat defects and faster re-indexing before a key sale event.

PPC · Search Terms

Amazon PPC spend clean up

Multiple campaigns with overlapping keywords, high ACoS, and wasted spend on poor search terms.

  • • Pulled search term reports and grouped by intent
  • • Cut non converting terms and added negatives
  • • Restructured campaigns around tighter themes

Result: Lower wasted spend and a cleaner base to scale from.

Launch · Readiness

New product launch framework

New products were going live with missing content, no clear campaign plan, and delayed keyword indexing.

  • • Created a simple launch checklist for catalog and ads
  • • Defined base campaign structure and naming rules
  • • Set up daily checks for the first two weeks after launch

Result: Faster keyword visibility and more stable early sales.
